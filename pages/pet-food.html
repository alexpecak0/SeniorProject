General Tips for Choosing Pet Food

  • Check the ingredient list for whole proteins as the first ingredient.
  • Avoid foods with excessive fillers or artificial preservatives.
  • Look for brands that meet AAFCO nutritional standards.
  • Consider your pet's specific dietary needs, age, and health conditions.
  • Consult your veterinarian for personalized recommendations.
