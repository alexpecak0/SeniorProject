Dog Care Information

Discover how to take care of the most common dog breeds in the world

Top Dog Breeds and Their Care

Labrador Retriever

Diet: High-protein diet with a balance of fats and carbs.

Activity Level: Very high, requires at least 1 hour of exercise daily.

Care: Grooming weekly, brushing teeth regularly.

Cost: $800 - $1,200 for a puppy, plus ongoing costs.

Vet Visits: Annual checkups, especially for hip dysplasia screening.

Common Health Conditions: Hip dysplasia, obesity, eye disorders.

German Shepherd

Diet: Protein-rich diet with essential vitamins.

Activity Level: High, requires both mental and physical stimulation daily.

Care: Regular grooming to manage shedding, weekly brushing.

Cost: $500 - $1,500 for a puppy, plus ongoing care.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups for joint and bone health.

Common Health Conditions: Hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, digestive issues.

Golden Retriever

Diet: Balanced diet with protein, healthy fats, and omega-3 fatty acids.

Activity Level: High, enjoys long walks, playing fetch, and swimming.

Care: Frequent grooming, especially during shedding season.

Cost: $800 - $1,500 for a puppy, grooming adds extra cost.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups for hip and heart conditions.

Common Health Conditions: Cancer, hip dysplasia, cataracts.

Bulldog

Diet: Controlled diet with limited treats due to tendency to gain weight.

Activity Level: Low to moderate; daily short walks recommended.

Care: Regular cleaning of skin folds to prevent infections.

Cost: $1,500 - $4,000 for a puppy due to breeding complexities.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups, especially for breathing issues.

Common Health Conditions: Brachycephalic syndrome, hip dysplasia.

Poodle

Diet: High-protein diet to maintain energy levels.

Activity Level: Very high, requires daily exercise and mental stimulation.

Care: Regular grooming, especially trimming of curly coat.

Cost: $1,000 - $2,000 for a puppy; grooming costs are substantial.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups to monitor for allergies and joint issues.

Common Health Conditions: Hip dysplasia, allergies, ear infections.

Beagle

Diet: Balanced diet, with a focus on controlled portions to prevent obesity.

Activity Level: High, requires regular walks and playtime.

Care: Weekly grooming and regular ear cleaning.

Cost: $500 - $1,200 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups for ear infections and joint health.

Common Health Conditions: Hip dysplasia, epilepsy, ear infections.

Dachshund

Diet: Balanced diet to prevent weight gain, which can lead to back issues.

Activity Level: Moderate, daily walks recommended.

Care: Regular grooming, especially for long-haired varieties.

Cost: $500 - $1,500 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups to monitor for back problems.

Common Health Conditions: Intervertebral disc disease, obesity, dental issues.

Yorkshire Terrier

Diet: High-quality dry food to support their small size and fast metabolism.

Activity Level: Moderate, requires short daily walks and indoor play.

Care: Daily grooming to prevent matting in long coats.

Cost: $800 - $2,000 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular dental checkups are important.

Common Health Conditions: Dental issues, patellar luxation, tracheal collapse.

Doberman Pinscher

Diet: High-protein diet to support muscle growth and energy.

Activity Level: Very high, requires regular exercise and mental stimulation.

Care: Minimal grooming, but regular exercise is crucial.

Cost: $1,500 - $2,500 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Annual checkups, especially for heart and joint health.

Common Health Conditions: Dilated cardiomyopathy, hip dysplasia.

Boxer

Diet: Balanced diet to maintain their high energy levels.

Activity Level: High, requires regular exercise and playtime.

Care: Weekly grooming, with attention to ear and dental hygiene.

Cost: $700 - $2,500 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups for heart health and cancer screening.

Common Health Conditions: Cancer, hip dysplasia, heart issues.

Rottweiler

Diet: High-quality protein diet to support muscle development.

Activity Level: High, requires regular exercise and mental stimulation.

Care: Minimal grooming; weekly brushing and nail trimming required.

Cost: $1,500 - $3,000 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups for hip and elbow dysplasia.

Common Health Conditions: Hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, heart issues.

German Shorthaired Pointer

Diet: High-protein diet for energy and muscle maintenance.

Activity Level: Very high, needs daily exercise and mental stimulation.

Care: Minimal grooming, but regular exercise is critical.

Cost: $600 - $1,500 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups, especially for hip and joint health.

Common Health Conditions: Hip dysplasia, cancer, bloat.

Shih Tzu

Diet: High-quality dry food to support their small size and energy.

Activity Level: Low to moderate; daily walks are enough.

Care: Daily grooming to prevent matting and regular eye cleaning.

Cost: $500 - $1,500 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular checkups for dental and eye health.

Common Health Conditions: Dental problems, eye issues, breathing problems.

Pomeranian

Diet: Small-breed food to maintain weight and energy levels.

Activity Level: Moderate, requires short daily walks and indoor play.

Care: Regular grooming to maintain fluffy coat.

Cost: $800 - $3,000 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Regular dental checkups are crucial.

Common Health Conditions: Dental problems, patellar luxation, heart issues.

Great Dane

Diet: High-quality diet to maintain muscle and prevent bloating.

Activity Level: Moderate; needs regular walks but not overly active.

Care: Regular grooming, especially brushing and nail trimming.

Cost: $1,000 - $3,000 for a puppy.

Vet Visits: Annual checkups, especially for heart and joint health.

Common Health Conditions: Hip dysplasia, heart disease, bloat.
